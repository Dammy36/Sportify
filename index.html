About the Artist

Adele

57,539,515 monthly Listeners

Since the release of her debut album '19' in 2006, Adele has become one of the most successful British Artists of her generation. Her acclaimed debut Album has featured emotional ballads like "Chasing Pavements" and "Hometown Glory." Her voice quickly set her apart as a powerhouse in the music world.


Adele was raised by her mother in North London after her father left when she was young. She showed a passion for music early on and was especially inspired by artists like Etta James and Ella Fitzgerald. She attended the BRIT School for Performing Arts and Technology, where other stars like Amy Winehouse also studied.
